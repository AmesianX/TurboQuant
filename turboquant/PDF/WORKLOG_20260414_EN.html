🔑 Tangent Residual Math Breakthrough

Observation: After Rayleigh Lloyd-Max, residual error lies almost entirely along the tangent direction (-sin φ̂, cos φ̂)

Analytic magnitude

r · π/16 — half-width of a 3-bit cell.
No per-block metadata required.

Direction is free

Obtained by coordinate swap of cos/sin already computed during dequant.

Cost

1 bit per pair (qtan[8]) + 2 FMA ops on read.

Effect

φ error ±π/8 → ±π/16 (22.5° → 11.25°).
